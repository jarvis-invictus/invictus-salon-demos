Our Philosophy

Slow Beauty,
Deep Roots

Sage & Botanicals was born from a simple belief: your hair doesn't need synthetic chemicals to be beautiful. It needs patience, pure ingredients, and skilled hands that understand its unique language.

Founded by three stylists who left mainstream salons behind, we've spent four years perfecting plant-based formulations with Ayurvedic herbs, cold-pressed oils, and botanical extracts. Every ritual is 90 minutes minimum — because transformation can't be rushed.

From Earth to Hair

Nature's Apothecary

Every formula in our studio is crafted from these time-tested botanicals — sourced directly from organic farms across India.

Amla

Rich in Vitamin C, amla strengthens follicles, prevents premature graying, and adds natural shine.

Bhringraj

The "King of Herbs" for hair. Promotes growth, reduces hair fall, and deeply nourishes the scalp.

Hibiscus

Nature's conditioner. Rich in amino acids that smooth cuticles, reduce frizz, and add brilliant luster.

Virgin Coconut Oil

Cold-pressed from Kerala coconuts. Penetrates deep into hair shaft, repairs damage, and prevents protein loss.

Rajasthani Henna

Triple-sifted, body-art quality henna from Sojat. Colors while conditioning, leaving hair stronger than before.

Brahmi

Calms the scalp, nourishes roots, and thickens each strand. A staple of our deep-treatment rituals.

Neem

Nature's purifier. Anti-fungal and anti-bacterial, neem clears scalp buildup and restores a healthy foundation.

Shikakai

The gentlest natural cleanser. Washes without stripping oils, detangles naturally, and leaves hair soft as silk.
