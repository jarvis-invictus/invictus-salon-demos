How It Works

From First Call to Wedding Day

A simple, transparent process. No surprises. No hidden fees. Just a calm, beautiful journey to your wedding look.

1

Enquiry & Date Check

Reach out on WhatsApp or call. We check our calendar, answer your questions, and share what to expect — no pressure, no sales pitch.

2

Trial Session

3-4 weeks before the wedding. We do a full trial, discuss your outfit, jewellery, and skin. You leave knowing exactly how you'll look.

3

Product Finalization

Based on the trial, we finalize products and begin pre-bridal sessions. Your skin gets prepped. Your look gets locked in.

4

Wedding Day

We arrive before you wake up. Everything is calm, organized, and exactly as you imagined. You focus on the joy — we handle the rest.
